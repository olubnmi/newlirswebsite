MARUF TIJANI

Director Finance & Accounts

Maruf Tijani holds a Bachelor of Science Degree in Accounting from Ogun State University, Ago Iwoye and is a Fellow of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (FCA) and an Associate of the Chartered Institute of Taxation. He joined Lagos State Civil Service in 1989 and has risen steadily through the ranks holding various positions in Finance and Accounts. He became a Senior Accountant in 1998 and rose to Chief Accountant in 2007.

In 2010, he was promoted to Assistant Director and in 2011 was deployed from the State Treasury Office to the Lagos State House of Assembly as Head of Accounts. He was promoted to Deputy Director in 2014.

Biography

Mr. Tijani is a consummate technocrat and has held senior roles in several government agencies as listed below; · Central Licensing Authority · Board of Internal Revenue · LASTMA · Lagos State Waterfront & Tourism Agency · State Treasury Office · Lagos State House of Assembly He was deployed to Lagos State Internal Revenue Service as Director, Finance and Accounts in September 2015. He is happily married and blessed with lovely children.
