PERSONALINCOME TAX

Tax is imposed on individuals who are either in employment or are running their own small businesses, under a business name or partnership.

CAPITAL GAINS TAX

It is a tax on the profit made from the sale or exchange of capital assets like land shares, machinery.

STAMPDUTIES

It is a tax imposed on legal instruments/documents executed by individuals, this tax is usually placed on the transfer of homes, buildings, copyrights, land, patents and securities.

BUSINESS PREMISES LEVY

It is a tax on property used for the production of income including rental houses, office buildings, factories etc.

HOTEL OCCUPANCY AND RESTURANT CONSUMPTION

It is a tax imposed on goods and services consumed in hotels, bars, restaurants and event centres within Lagos State.

WITHOLDING TAX

It is the specified amount deducted at source from payment accruing or made to individuals or corporate entities.
